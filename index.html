About me

A good website captures interest, holds it and makes people come back for more. Isn't that what you want?

I am a Web Designer/Developer with strong web design skills who's not only passionate about front-end design and site usability, but can also write semantic and reusable HTML/CSS that turn that design into pixel-perfect interactive websites that work perfectly on every device and mobile phone. I'm excited by new trends in design but know when to step back and work with time-tested solutions. I believe that beauty lies in the details. It's the apparently unimportant things that really matter, it's the size of a button, it's a font, it's a shade of the background. Due to my previous experience in photography I have a very good understanding of how things work together and at the same time I can make every bit of a website fit in the right place.
